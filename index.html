Learn to STRUCTURE your speeches and persuade the whole galaxy of your ideas.

Many great speakers use a single common structure that makes their speeches overwhelmingly persuasive. This web page will unveil their secret and teach you how to do that with 3 simple training tasks.

By the end you will learn:
1. What are the components of a persuasive speech structure
2. How to notice this structure in others speaches
3. How to write your own speech structure

A speech involves a MENTOR (you) and a HERO (your audience).A great speech is not about how you save the word; is about how you inspire others to do so.

TRAINING #3

Let’s build a structure for your own speech in 3 steps. At the end you can save it as a Word document to continue writing your speech with your nice new structure.

STEP 1

A great speech communicates ONE great idea, an idea that can change the galaxy's order. You can think your office, your school or a bunch of unknown people as your galaxy.

STEP 2

Let’s think about the dark side... What is the awful state of things that your idea aims to change.

STEP 3

Now bring your audience to the top of your awesome idea and show them the powers that they will have if they follow you!

Step 4
Your Structure is ready

Your structure is ready! Now, you can export your structure to your favorite Word and start writing your awesome speech.
